🍃 Our Story

Built on Love,
Growing with Purpose

Founded with a vision to create a loving and enriching environment for young learners, Feather Touch Foundation School focuses on building confidence, creativity, communication skills, and strong values during the most formative years of a child's life.

Our name says it all — we handle every child with the gentlest care, like the lightest touch of a feather, ensuring they feel safe, seen, and celebrated every single day.
